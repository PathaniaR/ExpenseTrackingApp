Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly ("we", "us", "the Service") collects when you use our personal expense tracking application, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly to us. This includes account details such as your name and email address when you register, and the expense data you enter into the Service — for example amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect limited technical information, such as your browser type and basic usage data, to help keep the Service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service. This means storing and displaying your expenses, generating spending summaries and category breakdowns, maintaining your account, and responding to your requests. We do not use your personal financial information for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time, subject to any records we are required to retain for legal reasons.

4. Third Party Services

We may rely on trusted third party providers to help operate the Service, such as hosting and infrastructure providers. These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their functions and are obligated to protect it. We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns and will respond to privacy-related inquiries as promptly as we can.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above, and your continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
